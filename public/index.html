Our mission

The good news of Jesus, supported by good technology.

Ministries and non-profits shouldn't have to choose between doing their work and keeping the lights on technically. FOSS Church removes that barrier — partnering with each organization to find the right fit, then delivering it for free or at cost.

Mission first

Every engagement points back to one thing: helping the body of Christ reach more people. The tech is a means, never the end.

Free & at cost

Services and products are offered free or at our actual cost — no markup, no upsell. Stewardship of your resources is part of the ministry.

Open by default

We favor free and open-source software so the tools you depend on stay in your hands — no lock-in, no surprises, full transparency.

Services

AI Consulting Services

About FOSS Church

A ministry that happens to be great at technology.

FOSS Church is a non-profit company. Our name says it plainly: we believe in free and open-source software, and we believe the local Church is worth serving with excellence.

We partner with churches, ministries, and non-profits who share our mission — to take the good news of Jesus to all. By offering technology free and at cost, we help those organizations spend less on overhead and more on the people they're called to reach.
